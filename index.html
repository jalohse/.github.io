About

Hi! My name is Jessica Lohse. I am a software engineer currently attending Ball State University. I also work on mobile and backend projects for the University through employment at the Digital Corps. In my spare time, I enjoy programming various applications, reading, and spending time with my friends and family. Feel free to look through my site and contact me at jessicaalohse@gmail.com if you have questions!

What am I currently working on?

  • A Look at Rendering Algorithms and Implementations for Mobile - My honors thesis for Ball State University.
  • Java Ray Tracer - To get hands on knowledge of rendering algorithms for my thesis, I am creating my own ray tracer based on Peter Shirley's Realistic Ray Tracing.
  • Ball State Map App - Updating Ball State University's iOS map app to iOS 8,fixing bugs present in previous verisons, and implementing a redesign.
  • Exceptional Me - An iPad app for middle schools students to keep track of their work.
  • Theatre Simulation - A web application using WebGL that will allow students to create their own stage set up for a production of A Midsummer's Night Dream.
